About Us

Since 2019, Ruchi Impex is a manufacturing and export unit based in Jaipur, Rajasthan - India, Recognized by Govt of India. Our company specializes in the production of high fashion ladies' garments.

We deal with fashion brands across Europe. From infrastructure, machinery, people, processes and delivery records, we are committed to providing highest standards.

Our Founder, Mr. Ravi Sharma started his special journey of hard work and success, back in 2001. He worked in many big export houses in Jaipur as Senior Merchandiser & Production Manager.

Today, we have a well settled small unit production at, H 170 Malviya Nagar industrial Area Jaipur, Rajasthan.
